FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

What areas do you serve?
We serve Flowood, Brandon, Pearl, Jackson, Madison, Ridgeland, Richland, and the surrounding Rankin and Hinds County areas in Mississippi.
How much does lawn care cost in Flowood?
Pricing depends on lot size, service frequency, and the scope of work. Standard weekly mowing in Flowood typically starts around $40–$60 per visit. Free, no-obligation quotes — call 443-676-3451.
Do you offer one-time services?
Yes. We handle one-time cleanups, seasonal services, pine straw and mulch installs, and french drain projects in addition to recurring weekly and bi-weekly mowing.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — Merritt Lawn Co. is a locally owned and operated business based in Flowood, MS, fully insured for your peace of mind.
How quickly can you provide a quote?
Most quotes are returned within 24 hours. Call or text 443-676-3451 or fill out the contact form for a fast response.
Best time of year for pine straw or mulch?
In Mississippi, early spring (March–April) and fall (October–November) are ideal — they protect roots through temperature swings and lock in moisture. That said, we install year-round.
